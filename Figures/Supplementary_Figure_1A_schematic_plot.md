
### Overview of the motif curation and refinement workflow. 
This plot was generated by using Adobe Illustrator. 

In total, 30,357 motifs were collected from 13 public resources and rescaled using a unified normalization scheme. Motifs were iteratively merged and filtered to yield 8,401 clustered motifs and 12,345 singleton motifs, forming the final "20K motif collection" comprising 20,746 high-confidence motifs.
