
### Supplementary Figure 7a, b
Both plots were generated by using [Metascape](https://metascape.org/gp/index.html).

> (a) Top 100 enriched pathways and biological processes for the 616 RBPs included in
the scRBP motif collection. Functional enrichment analysis was performed using
Metascape, and terms were ranked by -log10 (P value).

> (b) Top 100 enriched pathways and biological processes for 1,390 TFs curated from
the pySCENIC motif collection. Enriched terms were ranked by -log10
(P value) and visualized across functional categories.
