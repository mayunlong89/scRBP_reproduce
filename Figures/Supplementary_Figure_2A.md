### Schematic overview of the motif-scanning framework.

This plot was generated by using [Adobe Illustrator](https://www.adobe.com/products/illustrator.html).

Motifs from the “20K motif collection” were scanned transcriptome-wide using five strategies: Cluster-Buster (clustered), Cluster-Buster (unclustered), Cluster-Buster (archetype), FIMO and HOMER2. Scans were performed across four transcript regions (5′UTR, CDS, intronic, and 3′UTR) at both gene and isoform resolution. Motif-derived binding scores were converted into motif-target rankings, yielding region-specific ranking resources at the gene and isoform levels.
